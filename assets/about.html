About Us

Welcome to LifeLine Connect!

LifeLine Connect is not just a mental wellness platform — it's a compassionate digital companion for individuals battling stress, anxiety, depression, or emotional fatigue. Our mission is to create a safe, non-judgmental space where users can open up, learn, and heal.

🌟 Our key features include:

✅ A motivational homepage to uplift your mood.
✅ Animated sliding pages for About & Contact sections for a smooth user experience.
✅ A guided questionnaire that gauges emotional well-being and provides a personalized score.
✅ An interactive AI chatbot trained with mental health data, offering real-time support, voice interaction, suggestions, and more.
✅ Real-life success stories of people who overcame their struggles — to inspire hope.
✅ Emergency SOS contacts to instantly reach trusted people during a crisis.
✅ Peer Support network — a space where users can connect with others who truly understand.
✅ InspireReads: Curated articles that offer healing through reading.
✅ MoodMate: A dynamic emotion tracker using gauge/slider visuals to help understand your current mental state.

We're committed to building a future where mental health care is accessible, compassionate, and tech-enabled. Whether you're seeking support, sharing strength, or simply exploring — LifeLine Connect is here for you. 💙
